Crystal Game

Welcome to the Crystal Game! Rules are simple:

  • You will be given a random number at the start of the game. This is your Goal Number.
  • There are four crystals at the bottom that have a hidden numeric value.
  • Click on a crystal to add its value to Your Number.
  • Win the game by matching Your Number to the Goal Number without going over.
  • If Your Number goes over the Goal Number, the game is over and you click "Play Again" to reset.
  • Each time the game is reset, you will get a new Goal Number and the crystals will have new hidden values. Good luck!
